Lake Havasu

  • Lake Havasu is a fresh water lake on the Colorado River that sits on the boarder of California and Arizona
  • Being well known for fishing and boating, Lake Havasu brings in over one million visitors a year
  • The Parker Dam, located south of Lake Havasu, is the deepest dam in the world at 320 feet
